Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enable Dark Mode? expand_more
Go to Settings and toggle the "Dark Mode" switch. Your preference will be saved automatically.
Can I sync my tasks? expand_more
Currently, tasks are stored locally on your device. Cloud sync is coming in a future update!
How to reset my password? expand_more
Go to Settings > Account Security > Change Password. You will need your current password.
